# 🚀 Quickstart Guide to Neural-Forge-Ai on Hugging Face Spaces  

**Neural-Forge-Ai** on Hugging Face Spaces is the **preferred choice** for a **streamlined experience** in model training.  
This platform is **optimized for ease of use**, with **pre-installed dependencies** and **managed hardware resources**.  
It can be used by **both no-code users and developers**, making it **versatile for various levels of expertise**.  

---

## 🎯 Creating a New Neural-Forge-Ai Space  

Getting started with **Neural-Forge-Ai** is **straightforward**. Here’s how you can **create your new space**:

### 1️⃣ **Visit the Neural-Forge-Ai Page**  
To create a new space with **Neural-Forge-Ai Docker image**, visit:  
➡️ [**Neural-Forge-Ai Homepage**](https://hf.co/neural-forge-ai) and click **"Create new project."**

### 2️⃣ **Log In or View the Setup Screen**  
If you are **not logged in**, you’ll be **prompted** to do so.  
Then, you’ll see a screen similar to this:

![neural-forge-duplicate-space](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/v-punithkumar/Neural-Forge-Ai/main/static/duplicate_space.png)

### 3️⃣ **Set Up Your Space**  

- **Choose a Space Name**: Name your space something relevant to your project.  
- **Allocate Hardware Resources**: Select the necessary computational resources based on your project needs.  
- **Duplicate Space**: Click on **"Duplicate Space"** to initiate your **Neural-Forge-Ai** space with the Docker image.  

### 4️⃣ **Configuration Options**  

- `PAUSE_ON_FAILURE`: Set this to `0` if you prefer the space **not to pause** on training failures.  
  - Useful for running **continuous experiments**.  
  - If you **run multiple experiments**, this ensures they proceed **without interruption**.

---

## 🚀 **Launching & Training**  

- Once done, in **a few seconds**, the **Neural-Forge-Ai Space** will be **up and running**, and you will see this screen:

![neural-forge-space](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/v-punithkumar/Neural-Forge-Ai/main/static/neural_forge_space.png)

- From here, you can:  
  ✅ **Select tasks**  
  ✅ **Upload datasets**  
  ✅ **Choose models**  
  ✅ **Adjust hyperparameters (if needed)**  
  ✅ **Start training** directly within the space  

- The space will manage its own activity, **shutting down post-training** unless configured otherwise based on the `PAUSE_ON_FAILURE` setting.

---

## 📊 **Monitoring Progress**  

- **All training logs** and **progress** can be monitored via **TensorBoard**, accessible under:  
  `username/project_name` on the Hugging Face Hub.  
- Once **training concludes successfully**, you’ll find the **model files** in the **same repository**.

## 🔄 **Ensuring Your Neural-Forge-Ai is Up-to-Date**  

We are **constantly adding** new **features** and **tasks** to **Neural-Forge-Ai**.  
To **benefit from the latest updates**, update your **Neural-Forge-Ai space regularly**:

### 🔧 **Factory Reboot**  
Navigate to the **settings page** of your space and click:  

➡️ **"Factory reboot"** to upgrade to the **latest version** of **Neural-Forge-Ai**.

![neural-forge-space-template](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/v-punithkumar/Neural-Forge-Ai/main/static/space_template_5.png)

> ⚠️ **Note:** Simply **"restarting"** the space **does not update it**.  
> A **Factory Reboot** is required for a **complete update**.
